About Me

I am married to my college sweetheart and am the proud father of four rambunctious kiddos. I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Aberdeen. My focus is on political theology, the church, the kingdom of God, and social ethics. I teach ESL to refugees in Louisville, KY and am an adjunct professor at Bellarmine University. I am also the vocalist in the melodic hardcore band FALL.
